Our Story

Helpress was born from a simple frustration: finding reliable home service professionals shouldn't be so difficult. In 2020, our founder Sarah Johnson experienced this firsthand when a simple plumbing issue turned into a nightmare of unreliable contractors.

That experience sparked an idea. What if there was a platform that thoroughly vetted professionals, made booking services seamless, and ensured quality every step of the way? Today, Helpress serves thousands of homeowners and connects them with hundreds of skilled professionals across multiple service categories.

We're proud of how far we've come, but we're just getting started. Our vision is to become the most trusted home services platform, making home maintenance stress-free for everyone.
